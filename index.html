The Knowledge Base

⚙️ Technical & Connection
Can I join on Bedrock Edition?
Currently, Gearblock is a Java Edition exclusive network optimized for the best combat mechanics. We are looking into Geyser support for the future.
Why is my ping high in the Warfare maps?
Warfare maps use high-definition textures and fast-paced scripts. Ensure you are using a stable 1.20.x client and have "Server Resource Packs" enabled in your server settings.
Does the server support shaders?
Yes! Gearblock looks incredible with shaders, especially in the Nuketown and Baywatch maps. We recommend Iris or Optifine for the best performance.
What should I do if I get "Internal Exception: Connection Terminated"?
This is usually a DNS sync issue. Try flushing your DNS (ipconfig /flushdns) or restarting your router. If it persists, use the direct IP: play.gearblock.net.
Are mini-maps allowed?
Fair-play mini-maps that do not show player locations (entities) through walls are allowed. Any map showing player radars in Warzone is prohibited.
⚔️ Gameplay & Combat
How do I customize my loadout in Nuketown?
Visit the Armory NPC in the Warfare Lobby. You can swap primary weapons, tacticals, and skins using earned in-game currency.
Is there a level system for guns?
Yes! Using a specific weapon in Warfare earns you "Weapon XP," which unlocks better scopes and reduced recoil patterns over time.
How do I escape the Backrooms Level 0?
Look for the "Hum-Buzz" sound cues. The exit is a glitching wall segment that changes location every 60 minutes. Don't look back.
Can I build my own base in the survival world?
Gearblock focuses on Minigames and Warfare, but we have a dedicated "Creative/Build" zone for VIPs to showcase their designs.
How does the "Troll" system work?
Staff members use the Troll GUI to keep the lobby fun. If you're suddenly wearing a pumpkin or floating, don't worry—it’s just the v1.5 spirit!
Are there killstreaks in the COD maps?
Currently, we have 3-kill (UAV) and 5-kill (Airstrike) streaks enabled. We are balancing the 10-kill streak for v1.6.
💎 Ranks & Economy
How long do ranks last after I get them?
All ranks purchased or earned are permanent for the life of the Gearblock Network. No monthly subscriptions here.
Can I gift a rank to another player?
Yes. Contact a Founder (Allee) on Discord to arrange a rank transfer or gift for a friend.
What happened to my credits from v0.9?
Following the "Purge Recovery," all verified credits were synced with the new Vault system. If yours are missing, open a ticket with proof of purchase.
Do Founders get special permissions?
Founders have full network access and the ability to test new Warfare maps before they are released to the public.
How can I earn free credits?
Voting for the server daily and participating in weekend Hide N Seek tournaments are the best ways to stack credits for free.

We look for maturity over age, but generally, we prefer applicants to be 14+ to handle the responsibilities of the moderator rank.

Is "Trash Talking" allowed?
